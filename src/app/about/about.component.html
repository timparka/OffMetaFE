About Off-Meta

Off-Meta

Off-Meta started as a fun project during my coding bootcamp but quickly turned into something more. If you play League of Legends, you know that some characters are super popular and always get picked or banned. Off-Meta is here to give you more options. We suggest strong characters that aren't popular, so you have a better shot at getting your champion every game. I built this because I love the game and know the struggle of not getting the champion you want. My goal is to help you find a strong alternative, backed by meaningful data, that can help you win more of your matches. As for how it works, all the champion data is gathered and crunched by our backend. We run different algorithms to figure out which champions are strong but less popular, so you can be Off-Meta.
